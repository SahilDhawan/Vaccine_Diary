
# Vaccine Diary
Vaccine Diary provides a complete vaccine schedule along with timely notifications so that you never miss the vaccinations of your loved ones! A list of all the nearby hospitals and pharmacies are also shown in the application along with details like address, rating and their current status. Vaccine Diary provides a complete solution to your infantâ€™s vaccination needs.

Vaccine Diary provides details for each vaccination along with an easy to monitor calendar view. Get notified on a day prior and on the day of the vaccine. Set custom notifications time based on your requirements. Just log in with your email id or using your Facebook account and enter the birth date of your loved one to start receiving timely reminders. With an elegant and easy to use interface along with different color schemes for completed and due vaccinations you will never miss the vaccinations of your loved one.
